Precision in Model Rocketry

ArtySpace is a premium educational platform dedicated to developing skilled model rocketry enthusiasts. We guide learners in selecting the correct motor class, designing avionics using Arduino and Raspberry Pi, and engineering custom PCBs for advanced flight systems. Focused on propulsion theory, embedded avionics, and amateur aerospace certification.

Our Training Focus

Motor Class Selection

Understanding impulse classes, thrust curves, and selecting the correct motor based on mission objectives.

Avionics Development

Hands-on integration of Arduino and Raspberry Pi for telemetry, altitude tracking, and deployment systems.

Custom PCB Design

Teaching enthusiasts to design and build their own printed circuit boards for reliable flight electronics.

Certification Program

Level I | Mid Power Authorization

Authorization to operate mid-power motors with safety validation.

Level II | Advanced Propulsion

Advanced propulsion systems and controlled deployment mechanisms.

Level III | High Power Clearance

High-power motor eligibility with full avionics integration approval.

Achievements

Successfully executed a controlled vertical landing of a model rocket, inspired by modern reusable launch systems. Our testing framework continues to push innovation in amateur aerospace engineering.

Industry Connections

We collaborate and link our students to trusted vendors for rocket motors, airframes, avionics components, and recovery systems.
