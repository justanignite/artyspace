Motor Class Selection Program

Understanding Impulse Classes

Students are trained to interpret motor classifications from A-class beginner motors to high-power impulse systems. We emphasize total impulse, thrust duration, and stability compatibility.

Thrust Curve Analysis

Practical analysis of thrust-time graphs to determine optimal launch conditions, altitude targets, and recovery timing.

Mission-Based Motor Matching

Selecting propulsion systems based on payload weight, aerodynamic drag, structural tolerance, and certification level.

Safety & Compliance

Emphasis on safe handling procedures, launch protocols, and progressive authorization through ArtySpace certification tiers.
